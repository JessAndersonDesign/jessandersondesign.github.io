About

I'm Jess Anderson, a designer who likes to make things!

My design experience includes branding, marketing collateral, social media content creation, web design, packaging, layout, copywriting, and more. I am an experienced photo editor with product photography and styling skills as well.

In addition to graphic design, I am a crafter by nature. I love making all kinds of things and have a soft spot for plush toys and paper crafts!

When I'm editing photos I enjoy listening to audio books, podcasts, or music. When I'm off the clock you'll find me doing yardwork or watching British televison.

Thanks for getting to know me! Get in touch if you have any questions or would like to work together on a project.
