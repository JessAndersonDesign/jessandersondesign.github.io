About

I'm Jess Anderson, a designer who likes to make things!

My design experience includes branding, packaging, typography, illustration, layout, copywriting, and more. I have experience with product styling, photography, and photo editing as well.

In addition to graphic design, I am crafter of greeting cards, plush toys, and anything else I can think up. I have a craft blog where I share tutorials and ideas to inspire others to make things.

Two things that are always on my desk are my Wacom tablet for illustrations and my headphones for listening to audio books.

Thanks for getting to know me! Get in touch if you have any questions or would like to work together on a project.
